About Me

Hey there! I’m a Data Scientist with a Master’s degree in Data Science, and I absolutely love working with data. Whether it’s wrangling messy datasets, building predictive models, or creating dashboards that tell a story, I’m all in. My toolkit includes Python, SQL, R, Power BI, Dataiku, and a sprinkle of machine learning magic. Over the years, I’ve worked in Facilities Management and the oil and gas industry, where I’ve worn many hats—data analyst, data engineer, and even data governance enthusiast.

I’m also dipping my toes into Web Development—I know my way around HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m working towards becoming a full-stack developer. Right now, I’m diving into the world of AI agents and learning how to deploy them using LangFlow. It’s challenging but super exciting!

When I’m not knee-deep in data or coding, you’ll probably find me reading articles, books, or anything that sparks my curiosity. I’m always learning something new—whether it’s a programming language, a tool, or just a random fact that catches my eye. I believe that curiosity and experimentation are the keys to staying ahead in this fast-paced field.

Oh, and I’m a big fan of solving real-world problems with data and tech. If there’s a way to make processes smarter, faster, or more efficient, count me in! Let’s connect and see how we can create something awesome together.
